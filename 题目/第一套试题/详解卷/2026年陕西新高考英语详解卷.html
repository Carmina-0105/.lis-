56. 【答案】which
【解析】考查定语从句。先行词为"tea culture"，在非限制性定语从句中作主语，用which引导。

57. 【答案】are planted
【解析】考查时态和语态。茶树被种植，用被动语态；陈述客观事实，用一般现在时。

58. 【答案】to ensure
【解析】考查非谓语动词。此处用不定式作目的状语，表示"为了确保"。

59. 【答案】their
【解析】考查代词。修饰名词"quality"，用形容词性物主代词their。

【解析】考查副词。修饰动词"selected"，用副词形式。

【参考范文】
Dear Jim,

I'm more than delighted to hear that you're planning to visit Xi'an this summer vacation. I'd like to recommend the Terracotta Warriors and Horses as a must-see attraction.

The Terracotta Warriors, built over 2,000 years ago, is one of the greatest archaeological discoveries in the world. As you walk through the exhibition halls, you'll be amazed by the thousands of life-sized clay soldiers, each with unique facial expressions and details. It offers a fascinating glimpse into ancient Chinese history and the power of the Qin Dynasty.

To make your visit more enjoyable, I suggest booking tickets online in advance to avoid long queues. Also, hiring a professional guide will help you better understand the historical background.

I'm looking forward to meeting you and exploring Xi'an together!

Yours,
Li Hua
【写作指导】
1. 审题要点：
- 体裁：推荐信
- 对象：外国朋友Jim
- 内容：推荐西安旅游景点，说明推荐理由，给出游览建议
- 字数：100词左右

2. 评分标准：
- 内容完整（5分）：包含所有要点
- 语言准确（5分）：语法正确，用词恰当
- 结构清晰（3分）：段落分明，过渡自然
- 书写规范（2分）：格式正确，字迹工整

3. 高分句型：
- I'm more than delighted to...
